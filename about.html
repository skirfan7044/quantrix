Who We Are

Trusted Partner in
Industrial Automation

Quantrix India Private Limited is a trusted provider of industrial automation, motion control, CNC systems, and engineering solutions. We work closely with manufacturers, OEMs, machine builders, and industrial businesses to supply reliable automation products that improve operational efficiency and production performance.

Our portfolio includes motion controllers, servo motors, servo drives, CNC controllers, linear motion systems, PLCs, HMIs, industrial sensors, power supplies, and automation accessories. Every solution is selected to deliver long-term reliability, precision, and value.
