Product Overview

The Quantrix AC Servo Motor series is engineered to deliver exceptional dynamic performance across a wide range of industrial automation applications. Utilising high-energy permanent magnet technology and precision-wound stator coils, these motors achieve outstanding torque density, minimal cogging, and accurate speed regulation from standstill to maximum rated speed.

Designed for continuous industrial duty, each motor in the series incorporates built-in thermal protection, a low-cogging torque rotor design, and full compatibility with leading servo drive platforms including CANopen and EtherCAT fieldbus architectures. The sealed IP65 housing withstands exposure to coolant mist, dust, and machining debris found in real-world factory environments.

Available from 0.1 kW to 22 kW across multiple frame sizes (42 mm to 180 mm), the series is drop-in compatible with standard IEC flange interfaces. Encoder options include 2,500 PPR incremental and 17-bit / 23-bit absolute encoders, providing the resolution required for both general-purpose and ultra-precision applications.

Quantrix servo motors are trusted by manufacturers in CNC machining, robotics, laser cutting, packaging automation, textile machinery, and semiconductor equipment — wherever precision, reliability, and long operational life are non-negotiable.
